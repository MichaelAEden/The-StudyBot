Mitochondria is the powerhouse of the cell. Every human cell contains more than 700 mitochondria.
Mitochondria produces ATP, which is essentially the body's form of energy.
The ribosome is a complex molecular machine, found within all living cells.

There are three laws which all cells obey:
    - All cells come from other cells
    - Cells are the fundamental building blocks of life
    - All living things are made of cells

The human body is made 37 trillion cells.
